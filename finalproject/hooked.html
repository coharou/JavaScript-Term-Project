Hooked!

  ... is a 2D side-scroller where the player is equipped with a grappling hook, and tasked with flinging themselves over dangerous obstacles to reach the finish. There's one, extensive level without any checkpoints included. Being hit once is an instant death, causing a restart all the way back at the beginning.
