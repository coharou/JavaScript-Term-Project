Tic-Tac-Toe

  ... is the traditional pen-and-paper game brought to a digital platform. There are no differences in how it plays here. It is suggested to be played with another person at your computer or through a stream.

Controls & Tips

  The objective is to have three squares marked in a row - either horizontal, vertical, or diagonal - on the 3x3 board, all by one team. The first player will always be assigned the blue team, while the second player will be assigned the red team. To choose a square, simply press it during your turn.
